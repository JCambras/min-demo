Processing Agreement (DPA) covering the data Vercel processes on our
behalf (application code, environment variables, function logs).

Could you please send us:
1. Your standard DPA for execution
2. Your current sub-processor list

If you have a self-service DPA portal, a link would be great.

Thank you,
